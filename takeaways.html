Key Takeaways

Science is Technology. With the evolution of science, comes the development of technology. Technology is all around us. They are merely waiting to be used and to be discovered. We do not create tools themselves. We utilize the resources around us and refine it to suit our needs. Science is ever-growing and perpetual. Science will continue to evolve and improve in the future. Numerous advances and breakthroughs in science and technology will lead to the prosperity of civilizations. Science, along with other important variables, forms the foundation of our world.
